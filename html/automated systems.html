AUTOMATED SYSTEMS

UNIT 16






I choose 2 different types of automated systems and investigated them. One should be a basic automated (e.g. line- following) vehicle and the other should be a commercial system, e.g. controlling temperature or traffic lights. After choosing these 2 different automated systems. You are to explain the purpose of that automated system and the characteristics of the programs. The following 3 characteristics that needs to be explained. The first one is the features of it which means the attribute of that program whether is a hardware device or a control software program, the benefits of the program which means the advantage or the profit gained from that system and the last one is the reason for use (why is it used). You must also explain why these automated system that choose are used for like the reasons. After all these, you need to explain the strength and weaknesses of an automated system in one of the classroom.
